Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a photographer?

While your photographer focuses on stunning portraits and formal shots, I specialize in candid, documentary-style moments captured on mobile devices. The biggest difference? You'll get shareable content within 24 hours—no waiting months for your photos!

Do you edit the footage?

Yes! All photos receive light color grading and cropping for a polished, cohesive look. Videos are edited with music and transitions to create beautiful highlight reels.

What equipment do you use?

I shoot primarily on iPhone 15 Pro with a DJI Osmo mobile gimbal for smooth, cinematic footage. For the Ultimate Package, I also use a DJI drone for aerial shots.

Do you travel for weddings?

Absolutely! I'm based in Australia but available for weddings in Fiji and beyond. Travel fees may apply for destinations outside my service areas.
